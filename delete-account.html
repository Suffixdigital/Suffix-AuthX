Delete Your Account – Suffix AuthX

If you would like to delete your account and associated data for Your App Name, you can follow the steps below.

Option 1: Delete Inside the App

Open the app and go to:

Settings → Delete My Account

Option 2: Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion by sending an email to:

support@suffixdigital.com

Please include your registered email or phone number so we can identify your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • User profile
  • Authentication details
  • App usage data
  • Preferences and stored settings

Deletion Timeline

Your account will be deleted within 30 days of submitting your request.
